A Short Note on Fourier Transforms

June 2026

The Fourier transform converts a signal from the time domain to the frequency domain.

$$ \hat f(\xi) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x)e^{-2\pi i x\xi}\,dx $$

This lets us identify the frequencies that compose a signal.

For example: 


import numpy as np

x = np.linspace(0, 10, 1000)
y = np.sin(x)

Y = np.fft.fft(y)

The FFT efficiently computes a discrete Fourier transform.
