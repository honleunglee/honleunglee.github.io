Understanding Gradient Descent

June 2026

Gradient descent is the workhorse of modern machine learning [1,Theorem 1].

The update rule is

$$ \theta_{t+1} = \theta_t - \eta\nabla J(\theta_t) $$

where:

  • $\theta$ is the parameter vector
  • $\eta$ is the learning rate
  • $J$ is the loss function

For the simple objective

$$ J(x)=x^2 $$

the gradient is

$$ \frac{dJ}{dx}=2x $$

which drives the parameter toward zero.
